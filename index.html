About Atomic Cuber

Atomic Cuber is a Gospel Hip-Hop artist, rapper, producer, speedcuber, and creative dedicated to inspiring people through faith, music, and innovation.


Through powerful lyricism, energetic flows, and uplifting messages, he creates music that points listeners toward purpose and hope.


Beyond music, he is a passionate speedcuber, using discipline, focus, and creativity to push limits both artistically and personally.


He is currently a student at Allan Wilson High School in Harare, Zimbabwe, while continuing to develop his talents in music, content creation, and creative entrepreneurship.
